RULES AND GUIDELINES FOR BOOKING A ROOM

As the person making the booking you will be responsible for the room and for ensuring that all users of the room during this booking comply with Library regulations. You must be present in the room during the booking period. You must be a student or staff member at the University of Aalborg You can book a room for a maximum of 4 hours per day. You can book a room for the coming week after 12:00 on the previous Saturday.
