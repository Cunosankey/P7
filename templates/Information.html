Information

Five of the library rooms at Kroghstræde 3 at AAU are set up as group rooms or workspaces for project work. On this website, you will be able to get an overview of the rooms and the availability of these. The group rooms are available to all students at AAU so you are not guaranteed a room. For booking a room, go to the page Book Room.
Note that library bookings take precedence and your booking can therefore be canceled by a librarian in case they need a room for counseling and no rooms are available. In case they need the room, you will be notified a day ahead, and your booking will be canceled.
